Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the purpose of the marketplace app?
The marketplace app connects buyers and sellers, allowing them to trade various products or services in a secure environment.
2. How can I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the registration process.
3. How do I list an item for sale?
After logging in, navigate to your dashboard and click on the "List Item" button. Fill in the required details and submit the listing.
